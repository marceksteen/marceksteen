TITLE: Day in the rainforest

MEDIUM: acrylic paint on canvas

SIZE: 40cm x 50cm

I had a lot of fun painting this. Two wallabies wander around the forest floor, finding a beautiful little flower, highlighted by the rays of sun filtering through the trees. It's a cool day in the rainforest, and a cold stream bubbles along in the background. There are a few other little animals to be seen, amongst the foliage.
